A little about me...


The past 15 years have afforded me experience as a pharmacy technician, an EMT, a hospital corpsman in the U.S. Navy, and a perpetual student of science. Having focused much of my life on establishing a healthcare career, in the wake of the airborne sickness felt around the world and transitioning into civilian land I found myself emotionally and physically exhausted with patient care.

Though I still have a deep love for medicine, I have decided to give myself permission to pivot into a career field that will give me those same warm and fuzzies, IT.

IT has always been another love of mine (since the late 90s to be exact) and now is the perfect time for me to transition taking my problem solving skills, attention to detail, and desire to team build into another profession.
